Our Story

Fractal Labs was born out of another startup attempt dating back to 2017. Austin Wood (CEO Fractal Labs) joined the JustHive team as Chief Technology Officer – growing the team from 4 to 15 engineers in only 18 months. He searched relentlessly for the best mobile and backend developers around the world, and ultimately assembled a group that worked seamlessly together. When JustHive folded, the majority of engineers decided to continue working together to create the high-quality products we knew we were capable of. Now in 2020, we continue our startup journey together through Fractal Labs.
